OPERATIONAL FRAMEWORK

SIS employs a five-phase mission planning structure, ensuring clarity, adaptability, and results:

  • Strategic Planning: Threat assessment, objective definition, and resource allocation.
  • Preparation and Rehearsal: Scenario-based training, logistical coordination, and equipment procurement.
  • Execution: Precision operations using drones, small units, and localized assets.
  • Demobilization: Secure extraction, evidence handling, and post-mission analysis.
  • Evaluation: Lessons learned, after-action reviews, and continuous improvement.

Each phase is guided by six special forces principles: Purpose, Simplicity, Speed, Security, Repetition, and Surprise.
