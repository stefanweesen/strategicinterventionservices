TRAINING METHODOLOGY

Our training methodology is based on a structured five-phase model, ensuring clarity, efficiency, and effectiveness:

Strategic Planning

Define the training objectives, scope, and success criteria. Identify key stakeholders, resources, and constraints.

Preparation

Prepare training materials, personnel, and logistics. Develop training plans and ensure all systems are operational and tested.

Execution

Conduct the training according to the plan. Monitor progress in real-time and make adjustments as needed.

Evaluation

Assess the effectiveness of the training, gather feedback, and identify areas for improvement.

Follow-Up

Provide ongoing support and additional training as needed to ensure continuous improvement and skill retention.
