About Strategic Intervention Services

Strategic Intervention Services (SIS) is a specialized company that delivers drone solutions and associated services for defense, security, and tactical applications. Founded by experienced professionals with a background in military operations and strategic planning, we focus on providing high-quality, practical solutions for complex security challenges. Our headquarters is based in Georgia, with an international focus.

Who We Are

SIS was founded by SS and SP, two experts with a shared vision on transforming the deployment of drones and anti-drone systems in modern security operations. SS serves as the operational and strategic leader, while Nils is responsible for technical innovation and training. Together, we combine military precision with commercial efficiency to provide clients with solutions that are directly deployable in the field.

Our team consists of experienced professionals with backgrounds in defense, mission planning, and drone technology. We operate according to a structured approach, based on military principles and adapted for commercial applications.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip defense and security organizations with the best drone solutions and services, so they can execute their missions safely, efficiently, and effectively. We strive to set the standard for tactical drone deployment through innovation, reliability, and customization.

We believe in delivering more than just products. Our focus is on providing complete solutions, including consultancy, training, and continuous support, to ensure our clients are always optimally prepared.

Our Methodology

Our methodology is based on a five-phase mission planning model, combined with six core principles derived from special forces operations:

Strategic Planning

Clearly define objectives and requirements.

Preparation

Prepare equipment, personnel, and logistics.

Execution

Execute the mission with real-time monitoring and adjustments.

Exfiltration

Safe withdrawal and immediate evaluation.

Demobilization

Finalize and analyze results.

The six principles guiding our operations are:

Purpose

Clear objectives.

Simplicity

Simple, clear plans.

Speed

Fast and decisive actions.

Security

Protection of personnel and resources.

Repetition

Repetition of successful methods.

Surprise

Tactical advantage through unpredictability.

These principles ensure that every solution we provide is both effective and adaptive.
